Climb a Tree

You climb up a tree to avoid the dinosaur. It makes it to the foot of the tree and attempts to pull you down, but it's tiny pathetic usless T-Rex arms can't do anything. You sit there relishing in your victory. The T-Rex sits at the foot of the tree and waits.

You are stuck in the tree for the rest of your life, until you die of old age because you survived on rain water and a somehow limitless supply of Jack Link's Original Beef Jerky™.
